Our Story

Since our founding in 2005, Premier Real Estate has redefined luxury property transactions, specializing in high-end residences across America's most desirable markets. With over $2 billion in closed deals, our team combines decades of market expertise with personalized service to deliver exceptional results. What began as a boutique agency has grown into an industry leader, thanks to our uncompromising standards and client-first philosophy. We've earned our reputation through meticulous attention to detail, market intelligence, and discreet, professional service tailored to discerning clients. Today, we continue to innovate while maintaining the core values that built our success - integrity, excellence, and deep commitment to our clients' goals.
